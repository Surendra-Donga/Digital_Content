2. Problem Statement 
Design and develop a Full Stack Digital Content Rights & Royalty Distribution Management 
System to manage ownership rights of digital content (music, videos, articles, courses, etc.) and 
automate royalty payments to creators.

6. Business Rules (Strictly Enforced) 
● Content must be registered before usage tracking 
● Ownership percentage must total 100% 
● Royalty must be calculated only after transaction verification 
● Payment amount cannot exceed calculated royalty 
● Expired rights cannot receive royalty
